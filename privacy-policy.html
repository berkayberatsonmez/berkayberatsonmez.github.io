8. Data Security

  • All traffic to our backend uses TLS (HTTPS).
  • Server-side data is stored in Firestore with strict security rules; sensitive fields (credits, premium status) can only be modified by Cloud Functions, never by clients.
  • Local data is stored in SQLite within your device's app sandbox.
  • We do not collect passwords (we use anonymous authentication).

No system is 100% secure. If we become aware of a breach affecting your data, we will notify affected users in line with applicable law.
